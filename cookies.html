Cookies Policy

We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience, analyze performance, personalize content, and ensure secure access to our platform. This page explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how you can manage your preferences.

By using our platform, you agree to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You can manage or disable cookies at any time.

Why We Use Cookies

To maintain platform performance, ensure smooth navigation, and provide a fast, responsive experience across all devices.

To keep your account secure, validate requests, prevent fraud, and protect our platform from malicious activity.

To understand how users interact with our platform through anonymous analytics, helping us improve features and overall usability.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

Required for the platform to function. These cookies handle login sessions, security, and core navigation.

Functional Cookies

Improve your user experience by remembering your preferences, language, and interface settings.

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand how users explore the platform through anonymous metrics that power optimization and development.

Marketing Cookies

Used to deliver relevant content and measure the effectiveness of promotions or platform updates.

What Cookies Collect

Device & Browser Information

Operating system, browser version, hardware type, screen size.

Usage & Interaction Data

Pages visited, clicks, time on page, scroll depth, session flow.

Authentication Tokens

Session IDs used to keep you logged in securely.

Anonymous Analytics

Aggregated statistics powering design improvements and feature decisions.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can adjust or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they ensure platform functionality.

Some features may not work properly if you disable certain cookie types, such as login sessions or personalized settings.
